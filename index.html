Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most common dumpster size for a residential project?

For many home projects like decluttering or small renovations, the 10 yard and 15 yard dumpsters are the most popular choices. For larger projects like a kitchen remodel or roofing, the 20 yard roll off dumpster is often the best fit.

How does your pricing work? Is it a flat rate?

Our roll off dumpster rental prices are typically quoted with a flat rate that includes delivery, a set rental period (e.g., 7 days), pickup, and disposal up to a specific weight allowance. We provide clear, all-inclusive quotes so there are no surprises.

Do I need to get a permit for my dumpster rental?

If the roll off dumpster is placed entirely on your private property, like your driveway, a permit is usually not required. However, if any part of it needs to be on a public street or sidewalk, you will likely need a permit from your local municipality.

What items are you not allowed to take?

For safety and environmental reasons, we cannot accept hazardous materials. This includes items like chemicals, paint, oils, asbestos, tires, and flammable liquids. We will provide a complete list when you book your rental.

How much space do I need to clear for the dumpster delivery?

The delivery truck requires a clear, straight path to the drop-off location. You will need a space that is about 10-12 feet wide and has at least 25 feet of vertical clearance, free from overhanging wires or tree branches.
